# Users & roles

The **People** page is where a Platform Admin manages every account on the
platform. Open it from **Admin → People**.

## Adding a user

Open the **Invitations** tab, where the invite form is always shown. Fill
it in — including the person's initial role — and **Send invitation**
sends the email. The role can be changed later from the **Edit user**
page.

## The four roles

- **Student** — enrolls in courses and takes lessons and quizzes. Holds no
  management permissions.
- **Teacher** — views the analytics matrix and manages collections for the
  groups they teach.
- **Course Admin** — edits courses they own, and manages groups on those
  courses. Cannot create courses or manage cohorts — both are Platform
  Admin only.
- **Platform Admin** — creates a course and assigns its owner, then has
  full institution management: users, branding, access settings, SSO, and
  every Course Admin and Teacher capability.

Assign the narrowest role that covers what someone needs to do — you can
always widen it later.

## Changing a role

Click **Edit** on a user's row to open their **Edit user** page, and pick
a new role from the **Role** select. The change takes effect immediately;
the user does not need to log out.

## Deactivating an account

Deactivating a user (instead of deleting them) blocks sign-in while keeping
their history — enrollments, submissions, and authored content — intact.
Open their **Edit user** page and use **Deactivate**, or **Reactivate** to
restore access later.
