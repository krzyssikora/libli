# Users & roles

The **People** page is where a Platform Admin manages every account on the
platform. Open it from **Manage → People**.

## Adding a user

Use **Invite** to send an email invitation, or **Add user** to create an
account directly. Either way you choose the person's initial role; they can
be moved between roles later from their account row.

## The four roles

- **Student** — enrolls in courses and takes lessons and quizzes. Holds no
  management permissions.
- **Teacher** — views the analytics matrix and manages collections for the
  groups they teach.
- **Course Admin** — builds and edits courses, and manages groups and
  cohorts for the courses they administer.
- **Platform Admin** — full institution management: users, branding,
  access settings, SSO, and every Course Admin and Teacher capability.

Assign the narrowest role that covers what someone needs to do — you can
always widen it later.

## Changing a role

Open a user's row on the People page and pick a new role from the **Role**
select. The change takes effect immediately; the user does not need to log
out.

## Deactivating an account

Deactivating a user (instead of deleting them) blocks sign-in while keeping
their history — enrollments, submissions, and authored content — intact.
Use **Deactivate** on the user's row, and **Reactivate** to restore access
later.
