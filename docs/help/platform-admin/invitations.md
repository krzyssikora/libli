# Invitations

Inviting someone by email is the usual way to add a new account — open
**Admin → People → Invitations** and use **Invite**.

## Sending an invite

Enter the person's email and pick the role they should start with (see
[Users & roles](users-roles) for what each role can do). Submitting sends
them a sign-up link by email. If your institution has an **allowed email
domains** list configured (see [Branding & settings](branding-settings)) and
the address falls outside it, the invite still sends but you get a warning
so you can double-check it was intentional.

## Expiry, resending and revoking

An invitation link is valid for 14 days. From the Invitations tab you can:

- **Resend** a pending invite — this refreshes its expiry and re-emails the
  same link.
- **Revoke** a pending invite that is no longer needed; the link stops
  working immediately.

Inviting the same email again before it is accepted refreshes the existing
invite (role and expiry) and re-sends it rather than creating a duplicate.

## When an invite cannot be sent

An email that already belongs to an active account cannot be invited again
— manage that person directly on the **Users** tab instead. An email
belonging to a *deactivated* account is also rejected; reactivate the
account rather than re-inviting it.

## Adding a user directly

If you don't want to wait on an email round-trip, use **Add user** on the
Users tab to create an account directly with a role already assigned.
