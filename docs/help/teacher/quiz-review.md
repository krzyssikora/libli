# Quiz review

When a quiz includes questions that can't be graded automatically — open
responses, worked answers, anything needing your judgement — those submissions
land in the **review queue** waiting for a mark. The **Quiz review** button
opens it from the course builder — reaching it that way needs course-manage
access.

![The quiz review queue](static:core/img/help/review-queue.en.png)

## The review queue

The queue page has two sections:

- **Awaiting review** — submissions students have finished that still need
  marking. Each row shows the student and the unit, a badge such as
  **2 to review**, and a **Review** button.
- **Open (in progress)** — attempts a student has started but not yet
  submitted. Each row carries a **Force-submit** button (see below).

A count badge on each section tells you how much work is outstanding at a glance.

## Marking a submission

![Reviewing a submitted answer](static:core/img/help/review-submission.en.png)

Press **Review** to open a submission. On the left is a **Submissions** roster,
grouped into *To review*, *In progress*, and *Reviewed*, the first two with a
count, so you can move between students without returning to the queue.

For each question you see the question stem, the student's submitted answer
(read-only), a field for **Marks awarded** against the maximum, and a
**Feedback (optional)** box. Press **Save** to record your marks. A
per-submission badge tracks progress — for example **3 of 5 reviewed**,
becoming **Fully reviewed** once every question is marked.

At the foot of the page, navigation links take you to the previous student and
to the **Next to review**, so you can work through the queue in one flow.

## Force-submit

Sometimes a student leaves a quiz *in progress* — they never pressed submit, so
the attempt stays open and can't be marked. **Force-submit** closes and freezes
that attempt on their behalf so it becomes gradeable and moves into the review
queue.

- Force-submit one student from the queue's **Open (in progress)** list —
  this fires immediately, with no confirmation.
- Force-submit everyone at once from the review page's toolbar with
  **Force-submit all (N)**, which does ask you to confirm first.

Use it when a deadline has passed, or when a student clearly finished but forgot
the final step.
