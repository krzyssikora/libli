Notes are purely in-context (no notes list anywhere) — found via outline badges. Tags added/removed on units & filtered in the outline; a small "Manage tags" modal (opened from the outline's tag filter) handles rename / delete-unused. Lightest build that still satisfies the tag-cleanup requirement.

B

A+  plus a notes index (jump-links only)

Everything in A+, and additionally a per-course "My notes" list whose entries are just links that jump to the unit (you still read the note in context). Adds fast revision navigation — "take me through everything I flagged." One extra page.
