How much hand-holding?

A

Guided wizard + a "finish setup" checklist (recommended)

On first launch, the stepper above walks the PA through the essentials (all steps skippable). After/instead, a small setup checklist persists on the PA dashboard ("Add branding ✓ · Configure SSO · Create a course") until done. Best for non-technical schools; everything remains editable in Settings.

B

Wizard only

Show the stepper once on first run; afterwards rely on normal Settings pages. Simpler, but if they skip a step they may forget it exists.

C

No wizard — just good Settings pages

No guided flow; the PA discovers branding/SSO/cohorts/courses through well-organized Settings. Least to build, but contradicts the "easy for non-technical staff to start" goal.
